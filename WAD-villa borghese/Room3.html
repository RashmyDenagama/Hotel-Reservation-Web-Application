Grand Exexcutive Villa

This elegant and spacious suite comes with a living and outdoor dining area that opens to a private plunge pool and a wooden deck with outdoor seating and pool beds overlooking the stunning views of the Hunasgiriya Mountain Range and the memure. The room has a large ensuite bathroom with a shower, provided with hot and cold water. The suite also comes with a kitchenette equipped with cutlery, glassware, and crockery and includes a butler service to enhance the holiday experience. Guests can book this unit as a double suite or can be converted to accommodate a maximum of three persons with a single extra bed in the bedroom..
